About the System

MakimLida provides a secure, easy-to-use platform for student elections and voting. Future development will include authentication, candidate profiles, real-time results, and more.

How Voting Works

  1. Log in with your student credentials.
  2. Select the election/candidate you want to vote for.
  3. Submit your vote securely.
  4. View results when the election ends.
